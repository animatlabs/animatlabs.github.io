This Privacy Policy describes how AnimatLabs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit [animatlabs.com](https://animatlabs.com) (the "Site").

---

## Information We Collect

### Analytics Data

We use **Google Analytics** to understand how visitors interact with our Site. Google Analytics collects:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referring website or search terms
- Browser type and device information
- Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
- Anonymous usage statistics

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. Your IP address is anonymized before being stored.

**Google's Privacy Policy:** [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Comments

We use **Disqus** for the commenting system. When you leave a comment, Disqus may collect:

- Your name and email address (if provided)
- Your comment content
- Your IP address
- Browser and device information

Disqus has its own privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policy.

**Disqus Privacy Policy:** [disqus.com/privacy-policy](https://disqus.com/privacy-policy/)

---

## Cookies

When you first visit our Site, you'll see a consent banner asking whether to allow analytics cookies. Your choice is saved and respected on future visits.

| Cookie Provider | Purpose | Type | Requires Consent |
|-----------------|---------|------|------------------|
| Google Analytics | Traffic analysis | Analytics | Yes |
| Disqus | Comment functionality | Functional | No (essential) |

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some Site functionality.

We use the following third-party services:

## Data Retention

- **Analytics data:** Retained by Google per their data retention policies
- **Comments:** Retained by Disqus until deleted by the user or administrator

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of analytics tracking
- Withdraw consent for cookies

To exercise these rights regarding comments, contact Disqus directly or request deletion through their platform.
