## DORA Metrics

The four key metrics from the State of DevOps research:

| Metric | What It Measures | Elite Benchmark |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| Deployment Frequency | How often you deploy | On-demand |
| Lead Time for Changes | Commit to production | < 1 hour |
| Change Failure Rate | Deployments causing issues | < 5% |
| Time to Restore | Recovery from failures | < 1 hour |

## SPACE Framework

- **S**atisfaction and well-being
- **P**erformance (outcomes, not activity)
- **A**ctivity (but with context)
- **C**ommunication and collaboration
- **E**fficiency and flow

## DX Core 4

The unified framework combining DORA, SPACE, and DevEx:

1. **Speed** - Real velocity and deployment frequency
2. **Effectiveness** - Working on high-value initiatives
3. **Quality** - Reliability and maintainability
