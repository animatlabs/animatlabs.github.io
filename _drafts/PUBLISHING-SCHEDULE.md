## To Publish a Draft

1. Move from `_drafts/` to `_posts/`:
   ```bash
   mv _drafts/2026-02-03-efcore-5-query-patterns.md _posts/
   ```

2. The date in the filename becomes the publish date
3. Jekyll will publish it automatically on next build
